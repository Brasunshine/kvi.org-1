Why Study in Dubai ?

Apart from glamour, Dubai is known for its excellence in academics. In recent times, the Universities have been attracting students all over the world. In coming years, many new projects are being launched in fields of fashion designing.

 It would bring a drastic rise in job opportunities in these fields.

So it is recommended for students who have interest in designing to come here and explore more. Here students have many choices in programs.

There is huge scope in building construction and interior designing as well.

Universities very well prepare students for the professional world that they will be entering after studies.

Following are the reasons why students should study in Dubai;

  1. ⦁ UAE has well established Education System.
  2. ⦁ Universities in Dubai have modern infrastructure.
  3. ⦁ Dubai is crime free which ensures the safety of students.
  4. ⦁ Tuition fee and living costs are less as compared with other countries like the United Kingdom, the United State, European Countries etc.
  5. ⦁ It is considered as one of the fastest growing economies. So leading to more future opportunities. Students can dream for starting a job after their study.
  6. ⦁ Since students all over the world come here for higher education so they experience multicultural environment which is very valuable across the industry sector.
  7. ⦁ There are possibilities in finding full time employment due to vast development in the areas like Trades, Commerce and Infrastructure.

Eligibility Criteria

There are many Universities and Colleges where International Students can choose to study but they should meet the admission requirements.

Students require Study Visa to study in Dubai if they are not United Arab Emirates national or do not belong to any 5 GCC countries i.e Bahrain, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia.

Before issuing Student Visa, all the formalities of University must be completed.

Following are the documents required to apply for Dubai Student Visa: 

⦁ A valid passport.

⦁ Four Passport copies.

⦁ Recent twelve passport sized photographs with white background.

⦁ Admission Offer Letter received from UAE university.

⦁ Students Bank statement for the verification of funds.

⦁ A tenancy agreement. If Student do not have any, then he must get a letter from University if he will be staying in the University Accommodation.

⦁ Copy of tuition fee receipt for the current year of study program.

⦁ Copy of visa fee receipt for the current year of study program.

⦁ Post-graduate students need to show proofs of undergraduate degree.

⦁ Once student reach Dubai, he need to undergo medical test from authorized medical center. The test generally includes medical test and chest X-ray for HIV, Hepatitis C, Hepatitis B, Leprosy, Syphilis and Tuberculosis.

⦁ If any of these test results positive then student will be deported with the exception of Syphilis because it can be treated. 

                                                                                                   Dubai Study Visa Process

United Arab Emirates is a study destination for International Students. It has become International Education spot as there are many Universities and Colleges which are setting up campuses for International Students.

Students who wish to study in Dubai require Study Visa which is valid for full-time programs.

Student visa is valid for a period of 12 months so students have to renew their visa at the end of 12 month period if their program is longer than that. Students are given short term visas for programs less than 12 months.

Students must start the process 1-2 months before the semester. To apply for Dubai student visa, one must follow these steps:

⦁ Student must fill visa application form and attach passport sized photograph on it.

⦁ Make it sure that the attached photograph meets the required specifications.

⦁ Complete the application by attaching the required documents as per checklist.

⦁ Submit your application at Dubai application center.

⦁ On visa approval, collect visa copy from application center.

Earn while Studying

Part time job is a main concern for every International Student. United Arab Emirate provides off-campus part-time job opportunities to International Students. But students require permission from the University or College for part-time work. Students can also work on campus in Libraries, Department and Administrative Offices where they get opportunities to gain experience outside the classroom. During sessions, students can work for 15 hours per week or 60 hours per month. And during summer breaks, they can work for 40 hours per week or 160 hours per month.
