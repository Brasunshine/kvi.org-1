About KVI Desert Tour 

KVI Desert Tour specializes in tailor-made Desert holidays and exciting excursion in the sub saharan and other parts of the world for  

independent travels and private tours.

Through our strong international partnership programs,we arrange desert trekking as well as guided walks with night in nomad tent.

Why take a Desert Tour? 

If you have a place on your bucket list, chances are, a desert Tour will get you there. Our Desert Tour covers the world, taking millions of passengers each year to destinations like the Caribbean, the Bahamas, Europe, Sahara, Middle East. 

Our Desert Tour offers a tremendous value, with trips that may include your lodging, meals and entertainment, all rolled into one. Depending on your schedule and other important factors uniquely identified in a person case, we may  even offer fully inclusive vacations, which cover drinks and tours ashore.

we define desert touring as seeing the world while unpacking only once.  

Some of our Desert Tour also provide a wonderful option for families, offering things like waterslides, mini-golf courses and fun clubs for kids and teens

Planning is easy: Pick your destination and all the things needed for your trip.

Make an appointment online via email or you may phone-in at our online reservation centres and communicate details of your choice destination. We shall find the best deal for you within our ealiest convenience. 

Travelers say they choose to go on desert tour because it often cost less per night when compared with staying at a hotel, where they'll have to book -- and pay for -- all their extras.
