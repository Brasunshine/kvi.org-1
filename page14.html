KVI DONATION

JOIN OUR GLOBAL DONATION PROGRAM TOWARDS FREE RURAL EDUCATION,INNOVATION AND CAMPAIGN TOWARDS RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND ERADICATION OF POVERTY IN DEPRIVED REGIONS OF INTEREST TO KVI PARTNERS,FAMILY AND FRIENDS .

While sponsorship can help younger children attend school, it’s often too expensive for young adults to pursue higher education or vocational training without additional assistance. You can help fill this financial gap and help students achieve their goals by donating to KVI Education. 

For teens living in poverty, education is often a luxury.

Many times, those who are lucky enough to go to school have to drop out at a young age to help provide for their families. But when you sponsor a young adult, you create an opportunity to break that cycle.

And because Unbound stays with students as long as they are working to complete their education, sponsored teens have the chance to finish high school, trade school or college. Some even obtain professional and graduate degrees.
