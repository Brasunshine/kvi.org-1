KVI Internship 

Requirements

Applicant must submit photo copies of the following documents;

i.Passport Bio page

ii. Notarized copy of High School,University certificate or any other equivalent

iii.Notarized copy of Academic Transcript

iv.Two Passport size photographs (must be in white background)

v.Study plan (Short essay to tell us how you plan to spend your internship )

Guaranteed* Placement

We work with you to determine your career goals and interests and guarantee* that you’ll be matched with an appropriate internship experience in your field of interest in the city and term of your choice.

Full-Time Work Hours  

You'll intern for eight weeks, on average, over the summer or 12 to 14 weeks during fall or spring semester with hours that are typical of full-time work in your location, so you’ll make the most of the time in your new city.

Career Preparation

Before you go,KVI Internship Coordinators work with you to perfect your resume, cover letter, and interview skills so you’ll put your best foot forward with potential employers.

At the same time, you’ll begin the required internship seminar, which continues in person once you’re on site, to put your internship into context and prepare you for future professional opportunities after you’ve returned home.

Internship Programming Expertise

As a unit of Konnect Vision International Abroad, KVI Internships builds on more than 25 years of study abroad and 15 years of part-time internship programming to bring you a high-quality and well-rounded experience from a trusted organization.

Cultural Overview & Events

Our pre-departure advising, on-site orientation, internship seminar, language course, and cultural events will prepare you for and introduce you to the city and culture in which you’ll be interning. You’ll not only gain professional skills, but a new perspective, as well.
