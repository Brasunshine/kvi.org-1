About Us

Konnect Vision is a leading member for international Education and cultural exchange. We as a consortium ,provide a wide range of platforms for education,sports, international trade and business. 

We are currently affiliated with over 1500+ educational institutions as well as various governmental bodies worldwide to bring to you all the great and amazing packages of the KVI experience. 

 Mission

We advance the global mobility and integration of people into academic and professional settings by evaluating and advocating for the recognition of international educational qualifications and enabling individuals to fully utilize their education and training.

Vision

Konnect Vision International believes in a future where there are no boundaries to global mobility,where barriers to the u nderstanding or acceptance of academic qualifications no longer exist.
