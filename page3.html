Import & Export Consulting Services

The importing and exporting process can be a maze to those unfamiliar with the documentation, shipping procedures and various roles involved in the movement of goods from one country to another. 

 Even for the seasoned importer/exporter, doing it all alone can be a hassle. That’s why Konnect Vision international provides Shipping consulting services for our clients. Our import/export consultants have worked in every aspect of the business, and not only can we help advise importers and exporters, but we also provide customs brokerage and documentation services. A little advice can go a long way to help you get your international business up and running with fewer bumps in the road.

  Managed Services

At KVI Trade Consulting Services we recognize that businesses can’t always afford to employ staff with specific import/export expertise, that’s why we offer

 a cost-effective Managed Services platform designed to give you piece of mind while outsourcing import/export supervision to someone you can trust.

We provide effective and efficient Managed Services that support the day-to-day operation of your business. 

Working with clients across the country, through a mix of face-to-face meetings, tele-conferencing, and technological solutions, we deliver robust and compliant services that you can rely on.  
At KVI we understand that each business is different, that’s why we ensure the service delivery you get is tailor-made to your requirements. Whether it is a temporary solution, full-time support, or specialist in-house training for your staff, we can assist in all of your compliance needs.
By working with our team of experts you can take advantage of our knowledge and experience, from over 25 years in the industry, while implementing cost-saving solutions.
Find out how we can save you time, and money, on your export processes through our Managed Services contract.
