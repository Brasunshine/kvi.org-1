  iAgent 

 A Rewarding Journey 

Individual Agent; iAgent,is the term KVI uses to globally identify its vibrants agents across the globe.

KVI iAgents bring to your doorstep the KVI treatment of world-class amazing offers. 

Konnect Vision International is committed to working with quality agents who represent Chinese institutions as well as education institutions worldwide. Our work with agents, advisors, counsellors and consultants centres around promoting and supporting quality, professionalism and integrity. Training is a core component of our strategy.

With each client there is more learning, as no two cases are exactly the same. International student recruitment & the power of agents is very important to Campus China. You don’t have to look far to find criticism of education agents in the field of international student recruitment. From headlines condemning onshore student ‘poaching’ to accusations of application fraud, it’s harder to find praise for the role they play in helping students make one of the most important decisions of their lives. Maintaining integrity is essential for this controversial profession. 

Becoming a KVI iAgent !!!

  • 1. Notarized copy of Valid National ID. (scanned version).
  • 2. Notarized copy of Employment letter/affix photo and letter head of institution.
  • 3. Two Recommendation letters from your employers.(endorsed).
  • 4. Residential address as well as contact information(mobile & Telephone).
  • 5. Bank Account Information. 

Being an advisor who helps prospective students meet their future possibilities means having important responsibilities.

This involves guiding student from university enrollment to assisting them all the way to visa acquisition.

K.V.I will provide all agents who qualifies to serve as our agent offshore the necessary steps from scratch. 

We encourage you to be a part of this dream. Email us your information for the necessary scrutiny.

We are very vigilant on agents who request money or extra charges that are not part of what is originally prescribed by our unit. We welcome cooperation with all embassies worldwide to ensure that our agents don’t engage in any fraudulent activities. Student will be made to go through screening upon arriving here in china to ensure our agents comply with our operation code of conduct.

Konnect Vision International pays all it agents ranging from $50-$100 per successful recruitment. We also have a vacation package for each agent that is able to recruit 10 to 15 student per academic year. We shall provide you with a return ticket as well as documents to help you to personally apply for business visa to bring the student to china.  

Due to the important role agents play in their clients’ lives, preserving truthfulness and being upright with the people seeking advice is vital. There are certain practices that a consultant can observe to maintain the integrity in the entire process.

Most International universities don’t deal with agents offshore due to language barrier,trust and fraud etc. Please serve with all dignity when you are given the opportunity to represent KVI international student recruitment program as an iAgent.

We encourage you to be a part of this dream. Email us your information for the necessary scrutiny.
