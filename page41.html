About KVI Historic Tours  
KVI Historic Tours Inc, is our tour brand which specializes in which providing historically oriented vacation experiences for our guests through the operation of trolleys, street trains, high speed ferry, themed retail stores, festival marketplaces and other historic tourist attractions in the West,Asia,Africa and parts of America and middle east.

Our strategy is to capitalize on the Historic Tours of world brand, while focusing on heritage tourism. In this process, we will maintain our emphasis on guest communication and satisfaction, product value, superior vehicles and industry trends to profitably expand our entertainment business in each of the cities in which we currently operate and in other vacation destinations across the the Globe .

KVI Historic Tours finds it roots during the early 2000's in the partners’ shared passion for historic buildings.

This was our start in the Transportainment business.

 Our original partnership was formed to accomplish the renovation and restoration of buildings in Ghana and few other places across Asia. 

To transport retail customers to the restoration area along the city’s main street, the partners bought a company called Papaya Tours which consisted of two cut down bread trucks pulling a boat trailer with a metal awning roof and a plywood floor to which garden bench seats were attached.

Today, as The World’s Storyteller, we entertain over two million guests every year at our tours, attractions and historic adventures. Millions more patronize KVI history Tours as a channel to visit historic festival marketplaces and themed retail gift stores largely in China, and many villages and towns across the african soil.

KVI and partners Tour is thankful for every visitor to the great World cities such as; Key West, San Diego, Savannah, St. Augustine, and Washington, DC,Kenya,Shenyang,Harbin,Beijing,Fiji,Christ Church and Ghana where we are located.

Over the years KVI Historic Tours has grown slowly and steadily. Our invitation to" See The Best First" on our tours and attractions requests that you trust us with your vacation time. We know your vacation time is precious and our 100% guarantee* is our pledge that every Historic Tours of KVI CAST member will strive to Make Your Vacations Better!
